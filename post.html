Adopt A Senior or Youngster

This is my final project from the Web Dev - part-time coort at Ironhack

In a time that the elderly and the children are the most vulnerable due to the aperence of Covid-19, I tried to come up with a site that could be used to give senior citizens some break from isolation throug the action of volunteers or social institutions.

In the same spirit of comunity and volunteer work, qualified volunteers named "tutors" or youth suport institutions may register to help trubled teens gt a purpose or direction in life

This aplication has 3 schemas (User, Volunteer and Institution) that trough a set of rules may interact with each other. As explained in the image below

Build

MERN stack based project (MongoDB, ExpressJs, React and NodeJs). Googlemaps API, CRUD operations, validation and alert messages through React-toastify.
